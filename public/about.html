How this works


If you are a donor:

You can register yourself here and if a person finds that your blood is compatible, an email will be sent to your registered email address with all their details and you can connect with that person directly.


If you are searching for blood:

Go to the 'donors' page and in the search boxes enter your state, city and the required blood type and hit search button.From the results, choose the person you want and click on the 'request' button.Clicking that button will take to a page where you will enter your details like name, email address and phone number and after verifying your identity through your email address you can write a message to that donor and click the send button.Your details along with the message will be sent to that donor and he will connect with you directly.
